Tale Stars Features

X-Ray Autoshoot

Exploit

When you autoshoot while an enemy player is visible, your brawler will automatically target and attack that player. The attacks will continue to follow that player as they move, unless you switch targets to another player.


Anti Knockback

Exploit

Prevents your brawler from being pushed back by supers, jump pads and other stuff that would cause knockback.


Optimize Game

Allows you to optimize the game for overall better performance.


Chromatic Name

Visual

Allows your name to be chromatic like having a brawl pass


Unlock All Emotes

Allows you to unlock all matcherino winner, world finals (Only visible to you, work in battle) And all skin pins. (Visible to everyone, only works in team chat and club chat.)


Ranked Mod

Half-Exploit

Allows you to play any power level brawler 1-11 in Ranked


Nani Ulti Mod

Half-Exploit

Allows you to move Nani instead of the peep while using her super.


Old Rank System

Changes old ranking system tier 1-50 to rank 1-35 .


Theme Changer

Allows you change the main menu theme and loading screen with an older one.


Skin Changer

Visual

Allows you to change the skin of any brawler.


Spectators Menu

Visual

Allows you 1-1,000,000 normal or brawl tv spectators to your match.


Anti Auto Super

Disables Auto Super, needing manual aim to use super.


Hide Ulti Aiming

Hides super aiming so other cant see when your aiming your super.


Empty Pin

Half-Exploit

When you send the 5th pin in a match it sends a blank pin .


Battle Card Mod

Visual

Changes your battle card to Old Master or Pro Rank.


Auto Play Again

Automatically clicks "Play Again" after a match ends.


See Selected Brawler

Half-Exploit

Have the ability to see opponent's brawlers in friendly matches.


Status Changer

Lets you fake your status while in a team. (e.g., show “in battle,” “offline,” “creating map,” etc.)


Anti Autoshoot

Disables Auto Shoot, needing manual aim to attack.


Map Maker Mod

Half-Exploit

Unlocks hidden items you can place in your map maker maps.


Leon Clone Highlight

Half-Exploit

Highlight Leon's clones when he uses his gadget.


Spectate With ID

Allows you to spectate players directly by entering their player tag. (e.g., #2PP.)


Hide Battle State

Hides your battle state when in a match meaning your friends can not spectate you.


Random Spray Spam

Automatically sprays emotes in random directions around your brawler.


Chat Spam

Flood the chat by spamming a message you can set.


Decreased Cooldown

Reduces ping during matches.


Anti AFK

Disables the AFK message being able to stay idle for how long you want without getting kicked.


Remove Black Borders

Removes the black borders from matches.


Invite By ID

Half-Exploit

Lets you invite friends to friendly matches by their tag even if you’re not the team leader.


Font Changer

Changes the in-game font to custom fonts
